2023 Crypto Summer Lab

Sponsored by Polychain Capital and Lightspeed

May 22 - June 30th (6 weeks)

Austin, TX

The 2023 Crypto Startup Summer School, Sponsored by Polychain Capital, is announced. This startup school is an in-person event in Austin TX.

FAQ's

A. What are the costs associated with the school?

Response: The startup school is FREE for all participants. All lectures, facilities, housing and other resources are provided at no cost.

B. What material will be covered at the 2023 CSSS?

Response: The 2023 CSSS will focus on applied cryptography, infrastructure systems, gaming and related domains. Topics covered include zero-knowledge systems and protocols, multi-party computation, design of infrastructure systems and many other topics.

C. Is the startup school restricted to registered full time students?

Response: The 2023 CSSS is open to all participants, you need not be a registered student to apply.

D. Do I need a startup idea to apply?

Response: Yes, the 2023 CSSS is for startups, and you need to apply with a startup idea/pitch. Both individuals and teams with startup ideas are welcome to apply.

E. What stage(s) of startups will be accepted?

Response: We are stage-agnostic, and all startups will be considered

F. Is the 2023 CSSS in person? Can I be remote?

Response: The 2023 CSSS is strictly in person, and you are required to participate in person for the entire duration of the program. Remote participation is not permitted.

G. Is housing and other facilities provided to all participants?

Response: Yes, private co-ed housing (with shared bathrooms) will be provided for free for all selected participants at The University of Texas, Austin campus. Lecture halls, collaboration spaces and other facilities will also be available to all participants in the program.
