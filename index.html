2023 Crypto Summer Lab (CSL)

Sponsored by Lightspeed and Polychain

May 22 - June 30th (6 weeks)

Austin, TX

The 2023 Crypto Summer Lab (CSL), jointly sponsored by Lightspeed and Polychain, is focused on accelerating Web3/Blockchain teams, projects and startups regardless of stage. A hybrid program, we plan to bring up to 10 teams together this summer to collaborate with professors, crypto experts as well as VCs from Lightspeed and Polychain on new ideas in crypto.

Venue

The Indeed Tower in Austin, TX

All in-person events (such as kickoff and demo day) will be held in Austin's newest and finest venue: The Indeed Tower

FAQ's

Q. What are the costs associated with the 2023 Crypto Summer Lab?

Response: CSL is FREE for all participants. All lectures, facilities and other resources are provided at no cost.

Q. What are the deadlines/dates associated with CSL?

Response: Applications Due: April 15th. Acceptance Notification: first week of May 2023. Program Kickoff: May 22nd 2023.

Q. What material will be covered at the 2023 CSL?

Response: The program will focus on applied cryptography, infrastructure systems, DeFi, security, gaming and related domains. We will also help you with your business model and help think through your next fundraise.

Q. What are the deadlines/dates associated with CSL?

Response: Applications Due: April 15th. Acceptance Notification: first week of May 2023. Program Kickoff: May 22nd 2023.

Q. Who are the instructors/teachers associated with CSL?

Response: Our instructors include: Professors from The University of Texas, Austin; Domain Experts within the crypto community (from Austin and other parts of the world); Leading developers/programmers in Solidity and Rust; Business Strategy and Fundraising Experts from Lightspeed and Polychain.

Q. Is the startup school restricted to registered full time students?

Response: CSL is open to all participants, you need not be a registered student to apply.

Q. Do I need to part of a team to apply?

Response: Yes, all applications must be from teams, and teams must include at least one technical team member (coder/engineer). Only developer/coders/engineers can apply as individuals.

Q. Do I need a startup idea to apply?

Response: Although having an idea in mind is strongly encouraged, an idea/product is not required to apply.

Q. What stage(s) of startups will be accepted?

Response: We are stage-agnostic, and all teams will be considered regardless of stage.

Q. Is the 2023 CSL in person? Can I be remote?

Response: The 2023 CSL is a hybrid event; your team can participate remotely except for kickoff (start) and demo day (conclusion) of the event. For the start and conclusion of CSL, you are expected to participate in person in Austin, TX.

Q. What is Lightspeed's/Polychain's involvement in the 2023 CSL?

Response: Lightspeed and Polychain will be actively engaged in mentoring each selected team on their business model and fundraising strategy.
